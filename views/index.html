Live counter fresh from speakout for your deliveries

You want to display a live counter of your petition, but don't want to carry a big screen?

Fear not, we have the solution: put several activists together -one per digit you want to display, so unless you have more than a million signatures, 6 are enough- and ask them to bring their phone or tablet.

put them next to each other, showing their phone, each a specific digit. This little app makes sure they are nicely synchronised.

Ready? find the counter for your campaign, share the link with the volunteers and you're ready in no time. Have fun, change the world, we need it.
